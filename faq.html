Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
2–4 weeks is ideal. Rush orders may be possible—add rush fee.
Do you deliver?
Yes. Delivery is location‑based. Pickup is available by appointment.
What’s included with a mirror welcome sign?
Standard vinyl lettering and 3 hours of sign rental. Extra hours are JMD $500/hr.
How do I share my design details?
At checkout, add event date, names, color, and any Pinterest inspiration links.
Do you handle on‑site setup?
Setup is optional and billed separately depending on venue/time.
