About Glimmr

We’re an event signage studio based in Jamaica. We design and deliver mirror welcome signs, seating charts, and vinyl for DIY projects that look great in person and in photos.

Our Process

  1. Choose your product and place an order request.
  2. We confirm design details and timeline.
  3. Production, quality check, and delivery or pickup.

Traditional craftsmanship. Modern finish. Zero drama.
